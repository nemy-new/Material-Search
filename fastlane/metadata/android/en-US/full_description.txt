Material Search is a lightweight, privacy-focused launcher app designed solely for fast searching.

Features:
- **Fast Search**: Instantly find apps on your device.
- **Privacy Focused**: No internet permission required (except for web search intents), no tracking.
- **Material Design 3**: Beautiful, modern UI that adapts to your wallpaper.
- **Keyboard First**: Automatically opens the keyboard for immediate typing.
- **Shortcuts**: Customize shortcuts for your favorite apps.

This app is open source and respects your privacy.
